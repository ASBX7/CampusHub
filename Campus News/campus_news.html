Exams

Final Exam Schedule Released

The final exam schedule for the current semester has been released. Students can view the schedule on the university website or through the student portal. Make sure to check your exam dates and times to avoid any conflicts.

Exam Preparation Workshops

The university is offering exam preparation workshops to help students succeed in their finals. Workshops will cover study techniques, time management, and stress reduction strategies. Sign up through the academic support center.

Extended Library Hours for Exam Period

To support students during the exam period, the university library will have extended hours. The library will be open 24/7 for the next two weeks, providing a quiet space for studying and access to resources.

Exam Stress Relief Activities

The university is organizing stress relief activities during the exam period. Activities include yoga sessions, pet therapy, and relaxation workshops. Take a break from studying and participate in these activities to manage stress.

Exam Conflict Resolution

If you have a scheduling conflict with your exams, contact the registrar's office immediately. The university will work with you to find a solution and ensure you can complete all your exams without overlap.
