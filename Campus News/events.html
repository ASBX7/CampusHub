Events

Dance Marathon for Charity

Join us for the annual dance marathon to raise funds for charity. Participants will dance for hours, with breaks for games and refreshments. All proceeds will go to a selected charitable organization. It's a fun way to support a good cause and stay active.

Environmental Awareness Day

The university is hosting an Environmental Awareness Day, featuring activities and workshops focused on sustainability. Events include tree planting, recycling drives, and educational sessions on environmental conservation. Students and faculty are encouraged to participate.

Book Fair and Author Meet-and-Greet

The university library is organizing a book fair, featuring a wide selection of books for sale. The event will also include a meet-and-greet with local authors, book signings, and readings. It's a great opportunity for book lovers to discover new reads and meet authors.

Food Festival Celebrating Global Cuisine

The university is hosting a food festival celebrating global cuisine. The event will feature food stalls offering dishes from various cultures, live cooking demonstrations, and cultural performances. It's a delicious way to experience the diversity of our campus community.

Theater Production of Classic Play

The university's theater department is putting on a production of a classic play. The performance will feature talented student actors and a beautifully designed set. Tickets are available for purchase, and all are welcome to attend.
